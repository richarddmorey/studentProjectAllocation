Introduction

This app is for the allocation of students to projects. It is an implementation of Abraham et al‚Äôs (2007) SPA-student algorithm:

üìÑAbraham, D.J., Irving, R.W., and Manlove, D.M. (2007) Two algorithms for the student-project allocation problem. Journal of Discrete Algorithms, 5(1), pp. 73-90. (doi:10.1016/j.jda.2006.03.006)

The algorithm assigns students to projects, given preferences of students for projects, the offerings of projects by lecturers, preferences of lecturers for students, and respecting limits on lecturer and project loads.
